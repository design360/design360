What is Design 360?



Design 360 is a designathon where the teams give solutions to critical problems through design, prototyping, and testing ideas. Our mission is to bring together creative thinkers, designers and UI/UX enthusiasts to build things that shape our future. During the event, the students will be required to submit their idea and an interface/vid interface/video, followed by a presentation.

25th - 26th August at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi

Okhla Industrial Estate, Phase III, Near Govind Puri Metro Station, Delhi, 110020

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can come?

All school and college students are allowed. Just make sure that you get a valid student ID.

How much does it cost?

Design 360 costs nothing. Registering and attending is free, and you get free food, prizes, swag and a great learning experience.

What should I bring?

Bring your valid student ID, laptop, phones, charger, wires, camera, etc. (we will be providing internet, mattresses, food and coffee).

When and where is it?

Our event will be held on August 25th to 26th as a 16 hour overnight event. It will take place inside IIIT Delhi in our newly constructed academic block!

Can I have an example problem statement?

Previous problem statements can be viewed here. Projects created by our participants last time can be found here.

How do I form a team?

You'll have time to find a team when you arrive. If you already have a team, just list your teammates when you register.

What about accomodation?

We will make special arrangements in case you arrive a day before the event. Just shoot an email to one of the organizers.

What can I win?

We're working with some awesome companies to give out tons of prizes. Check back later to know more.
