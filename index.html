What is Design 360?



Design 360 is a design based hackathon where the teams give solutions to critical problems through design, prototyping, and testing ideas. Our mission is to bring together creative thinkers, designers and UI/UX enthusiasts to build things that shape our future. During the event, the students will be required to submit their idea and an interface/vid interface/video, followed by a presentation.

25th - 26th August at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi

Okhla Industrial Estate, Phase III, Near Govind Puri Metro Station, Delhi, 110020

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can come?

All school and college students are allowed. Just make sure that you get a valid student ID.

How much does it cost?

Design 360 costs nothing. Registering and attending is free, and you get free food, prizes, swag and a great learning experience.

What should I bring?

Bring your valid student ID, laptop, phones, charger, wires, camera, etc. (we will be providing mattress, food and coffee).

When and where is it?

August 25-26, 2017: Friday to Saturday, overnight at IIIT Delhi, Okhla Phase III, in our newly constructed academic block!
